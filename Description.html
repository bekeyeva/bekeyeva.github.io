DESCRIPTION

"This is my ninja way!»

The birthday of Naruto Uzumaki on the village called Konoha was attacked by the legendary demon, Nine-tailed Fox. The fourth Hokage at the cost of his life saved the village, sealing the demon in the newborn Naruto, unknowingly dooming him to a life of hatred of the villagers. Despite the lack of talent in many areas of ninjutsu, restlessness and cockiness, Naruto has a dream — to become Hokage, the strongest ninja in the village. Wanting recognition, which did not receive, he works hard and trains with his team-mates, Sasuke with Uchihas and Sakura Haruno and their mentor Kakashi, Hatake. He and his partners will have to go through a lot on the way to their cherished places: battles, love, friendship, betrayal, thirst for power...
